Harry Potter the boy who lived!

Harry Potter

Harry is described as the boy who lived. Early on Harry's parents died while he lived after being struck by Lord Voldermort. The story follows him and his 2 best friends after he finds out he comes from a famous wizard family. He goes to a wizarding school despite his aunt and uncles disapproval, finds Quidditch, and learns how to be a wizard.

Ron Weasley

Ron is described as Harry's best friend and also comes from a long line of wizarding parents called pure bloods. Unlike Harry, Ron comes from a poor family. He is goofy, extremley worried all the time, and always stays loyal to his friends and family.

Hermione Granger

Hermione comes from a muggle family (meaning her parents aren't wizards). She is overly confident when it comes to knowing her wizarding facts and often is the one with all of the answers. She is brave and often known as a know it all.
